About GoldSecurity Learning

This website serves as an assessment tool designed to help users analyze and improve their cybersecurity knowledge efficiently. This assessment tool was designed with emphasis on the elderly population. You are guaranteed to learn everything you need to learn after completing all sections and its assessments.

Our Aims:

  • Provide structured assessment tools that aim to help the elderly learn about security risks
  • Test users on section topics to reinforce their knowledge and simulate real-world experiences
  • Help users track progress throughout the different sections and assessments
  • Make an impact on the elderly population and spread awareness about cybersecurity importance
